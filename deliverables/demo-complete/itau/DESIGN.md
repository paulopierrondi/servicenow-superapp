# Itaú ServiceNow Super App Complete Demo

## Style Prompt

Vertical executive mobile demo in Apple-style product motion. The app should feel like a high-end Brazilian banking mobile experience, but the work surface is ServiceNow-first: Now Assist, ITSM, SPM, CSM, CRM, CMDB Health, AI Control Tower and autonomous workflow. Keep the UI calm, legible and premium. Avoid a busy dashboard wall.

## Colors

- Brand accent: #EC7000
- Brand dark accent: #B34A00
- Secondary operational accent: #003399
- iOS grouped background: #FFF7EF
- Card surface: #FFFFFF
- Primary ink: #171717

## Typography

- Primary: Apple system stack, SF Pro compatible.
- Use large headings only for scene titles. In-app panels use compact, scannable text.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not turn the bank into a transaction demo.
- Do not use generic SaaS purple gradients.
- Do not create dense unreadable dashboards.
- Do not imply autonomous execution without human approval.
- Do not show real personal or financial data.
